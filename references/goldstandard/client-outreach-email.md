# Gold Standard — Client Outreach Email

*Tone: professional but warm. No jargon. No filler. Gets to the point fast.*

---

**Subject:** Saving your team 10 hours a week — worth a quick call?

Hi [Name],

I noticed [Company] is growing fast — which usually means your team is juggling more than they should be.

At NEXA Consulting, we build automated workflows that handle the repetitive side of operations — onboarding, data collection, reporting — so your team can focus on the work that actually moves the needle.

We've done this for HR and professional services firms similar to yours. Results come fast, usually within the first two weeks.

Would a 20-minute call this week make sense? Happy to work around your schedule.

Ikram
Founder, NEXA Consulting

---

*What makes this work:*
- Opens with them, not us
- States the value in plain language — no buzzwords
- Social proof in one line, no over-selling
- Ask is low-commitment and specific
- Closes warmly, not formally
