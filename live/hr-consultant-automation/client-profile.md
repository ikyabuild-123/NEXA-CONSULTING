## What They Do

Premium HR consultancy covering:
- **Executive search / headhunting** — leadership and management roles
- **Recruitment** — specialist hires across departments
- **Employer branding / personnel marketing** — multi-channel: LinkedIn, XING, Instagram, TikTok, job boards
- **HR tools and digitalization** — assessment centres, employee surveys, payroll

**Market:** Mid-size companies, public sector, European firms entering Germany. Geography: D.A.CH. (Germany, Austria, Switzerland).

---

## How They Work

Relationship-driven, high-touch. Three-phase process: Longlist → Shortlist (3–5 profiles) → Contract closure. They do active sourcing (outbound to candidates) not passive advertising. ISO-certified, psychologically rigorous.
